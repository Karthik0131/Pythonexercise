Concatenate Two Lists in the Following Order

Given: list1 = ["Hello ", "take "], list2 = ["Dear", "Sir"]

Expected output: ['Hello Dear', 'Hello Sir', 'take Dear', 'take Sir']

list1 = ["Hello ", "take "]
list2 = ["Dear", "Sir"]

res = [x + y for x in list1 for y in list2]
print(res)
