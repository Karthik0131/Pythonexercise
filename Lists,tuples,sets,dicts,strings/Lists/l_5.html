Iterate Both Lists Simultaneously

Given: list1 = [10, 20, 30, 40], list2 = [100, 200, 300, 400]

Expected output: 10 400, 20 300, 30 200, 40 100

list1 = [10, 20, 30, 40]
list2 = [100, 200, 300, 400]

for x, y in zip(list1, list2[::-1]):
    print(x, y)
